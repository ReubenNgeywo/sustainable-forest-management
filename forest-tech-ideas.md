# Technology Ideas for Better Forest Management

## 1. Satellite + AI monitoring
- Near real-time deforestation alerts (Global Forest Watch, GLAD, Hansen data)
- PlanetScope / Sentinel-2 change detection

## 2. Drones + LiDAR
- Detailed canopy structure mapping
- Early pest/disease detection
- Precision reforestation seed dropping

## 3. IoT ground sensors
- Soil moisture + temperature networks
- Early wildfire smoke/heat detectors
- Chainsaw/gunshot acoustic sensors

## 4. Acoustic + camera AI
- Biodiversity monitoring via bird/insect calls
- Camera trap species identification
- Poaching hotspot prediction

## 5. Blockchain timber tracking
- Legal supply chain transparency (stump → mill → consumer)

## 6. Predictive ML models
- Fire danger forecasting (7–30 days)
- Illegal logging risk mapping
- Carbon stock estimation
